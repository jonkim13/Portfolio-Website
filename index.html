Projects

DUI Risk Radar

Awarded 2nd place at BorderHacks, DUI Risk Radar supports UN Goal 3.6 by visualizing DUI accident hotspots with Google Maps heatmaps. The web app alerts users of potential DUI-prone areas along their route, aiming to reduce traffic-related deaths and injuries.

Snap Market

Created for the TikTok TechJam hackathon, this web app enables users to upload media like TikTok videos and use a screenshot function to search for products featured in the video, inspired by Google Pixel Vision.

Poke Generator

A custom fine-tuned Stable Diffusion 2 model crafted to generate Pokémon that accurately resemble specified characters, addressing the limitations of the original model, which lacked specific Pokémon data.
